[X] Head Command List

[\] head file.txt
[/] Show first 10 lines

[\] head -n 20 file.txt
[/] Show first 20 lines

[\] head -c 100 file.txt
[/] Show first 100 bytes

[\] head -q file1 file2
[/] Show first 10 lines of multiple files without headers

[\] head -v file1 file2
[/] Show filenames before output when multiple files

[\] head --help
[/] Show head help menu
