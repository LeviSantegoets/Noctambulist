[/] Scan available SDR devices

[/] List connected SDR devices

[/] Analyze captured signal file

[\] gr-baz plot-spectrogram output.bin
[/] Display spectrogram of captured signals

[\] gr-baz demodulate output.bin --mode fm
[/] Demodulate FM signal from capture

[\] gr-baz demodulate output.bin --mode am
[/] Demodulate AM signal from capture

[\] gr-baz freq-scan 88M-108M
[/] Scan frequency range (example: FM band)

[\] gr-baz gain -d rtl0 -g 20
[/] Set gain for SDR device

[\] gr-baz info -d rtl0
[/] Show detailed info about SDR device

[\] gr-baz --interactive
[/] Launch interactive mode for live analysis
