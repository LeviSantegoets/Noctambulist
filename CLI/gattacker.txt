[X] Gattacker Command List

[\] gattacker scan
[/] Scan for nearby BLE devices

[\] gattacker connect <MAC>
[/] Connect to a BLE device by MAC address

[\] gattacker disconnect <MAC>
[/] Disconnect from a BLE device

[\] gattacker services <MAC>
[/] List all GATT services of a device

[\] gattacker characteristics <MAC> <serviceUUID>
[/] List characteristics of a specific service

[\] gattacker read <MAC> <charUUID>
[/] Read value from a characteristic

[\] gattacker write <MAC> <charUUID> <value>
[/] Write value to a characteristic

[\] gattacker notify <MAC> <charUUID>
[/] Subscribe to notifications from a characteristic

[\] gattacker spoof <MAC> <charUUID> <value>
[/] Spoof characteristic value

[\] gattacker mitm <MAC>
[/] Perform MITM attack on BLE device (if supported)

[\] gattacker log
[/] Log BLE traffic

[\] gattacker -h
[/] Show help menu

[\] gattacker --version
[/] Show version
